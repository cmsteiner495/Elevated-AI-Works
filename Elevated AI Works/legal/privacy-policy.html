Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Elevated AI Works (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website or work together on a project.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact form details: name, email address, phone number (if provided), and the details you share about your project.
  • Email communication: messages you send and replies related to your project or inquiries.
  • Basic usage information: general, non-identifying data such as page views or referral sources, typically through standard analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used primarily to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes or proposals.
  • Plan, create, and deliver project work.
  • Maintain basic records for bookkeeping and client history.
  • Improve services and understand what people are most interested in.

3. Email & Communication

If you contact Elevated AI Works, you may receive follow-up emails related to your project, questions, or potential work together. You can ask to stop receiving non-essential messages at any time by replying and requesting that communication be limited or ended.

4. Sharing of Information

Your information is not sold or rented to third parties. It may be shared with trusted service providers only when reasonably necessary to:

  • Send or receive emails and files.
  • Process payments (if applicable).
  • Host the website and related forms.

These providers are expected to handle data responsibly, but they have their own privacy policies and systems.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Project-related communication and files may be stored for a reasonable period for recordkeeping, portfolio reference, and future work together. If you would like certain information deleted after a project, you can request that in writing, and reasonable efforts will be made to honor that request where practical.

6. Cookies & Analytics

This site may use basic analytics or performance tools that rely on cookies or similar technologies. These are generally used to understand which pages are visited and to keep the site running smoothly. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

The website may link to other sites (for example, form providers, portfolio platforms, or social media). Elevated AI Works is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

8. Children’s Privacy

This site and its services are not directed toward children under 13, and personal information about children is not knowingly collected. If you believe information about a child has been provided, please get in touch so it can be addressed.

9. Your Choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information by contacting Elevated AI Works. Some records may need to be retained for legal, tax, or business reasons, but reasonable steps will be taken to honor appropriate requests.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the current version. Continued use of the site or services after changes are posted indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the contact page.
