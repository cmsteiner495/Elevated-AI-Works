Refund & Revision Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Refund & Revision Policy explains how payments, revisions, cancellations, and refunds are generally handled for projects with Elevated AI Works (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Because each project is unique, specific terms in your proposal or invoice may supplement or clarify the points below.

1. Deposits & Project Start

Many projects begin with a deposit or initial payment. Deposits are typically non-refundable once work has started, as time is scheduled and initial work is created based on your project.

2. Included Revisions

Each project includes a defined number of revision rounds for major deliverables (for example, logo concepts or page layouts). Revisions are meant for adjustments and refinements, not full restarts.

Examples of what counts as a revision round:

  • Tweaking colors, fonts, or layout details.
  • Adjusting text placement or imagery selection.
  • Combining preferred elements from earlier options.

Completely new directions outside the original brief may be considered new work and quoted separately.

3. Additional Changes & Out-of-Scope Work

If you request changes that go beyond the agreed scope or the included revision rounds, you may be offered:

  • An updated quote for the extra work, or
  • A smaller scoped adjustment if you prefer to keep things simple.

4. Cancellations by the Client

If you choose to cancel a project after work has started:

  • Any non-refundable deposit remains non-refundable.
  • Additional payment may be due to cover work completed up to the cancellation date.

You will generally receive any work completed and paid for up to that point, in whatever stage it is in.

5. Cancellations by Elevated AI Works

In rare cases (for example, unforeseen personal circumstances or ethical conflicts), Elevated AI Works may need to cancel a project. If that happens, reasonable efforts will be made to:

  • Refund any payment for work not yet performed, and
  • Deliver usable work-in-progress files when appropriate.

6. Refund Requests

Because design and digital work are largely custom and time-based, full refunds are not typically offered after significant progress has been made. However, if something feels off or expectations are not being met, you’re encouraged to communicate that as early as possible so adjustments can be made.

7. Turnaround & Delays

Timelines are based on both sides staying reasonably responsive. Delays in feedback, approvals, or content may push back delivery dates. Elevated AI Works is not responsible for delays caused by late responses or incomplete information.

8. Final Approval

Before final files are delivered or published, you will have an opportunity to review and approve the work. Once approved and delivered, further changes may be treated as a new phase or mini-project.

9. Questions & Exceptions

Life happens, and not every situation fits neatly into a policy. If something unexpected comes up, Elevated AI Works will aim to handle it with fairness and clear communication. If you have questions about how this policy applies to your project, please ask.

For any questions about this Refund & Revision Policy, reach out through the contact page.
