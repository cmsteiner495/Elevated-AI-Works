Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: [November 25, 2025]

Elevated AI Works provides custom, service-based work. This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how deposits, cancellations, and refunds are handled for our projects.

1. General Policy

By approving a proposal, paying an invoice, or otherwise engaging our Services, you agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy in addition to our Terms of Service.

2. Deposits & Upfront Payments

  • Deposits or upfront payments are generally non-refundable once work has begun.
  • Deposits reserve time in our schedule and cover initial planning, communication, and early creative work.

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 When a Refund May Be Considered

A partial or full refund may be considered if:

  • No work has yet been started on your project and you contact us promptly after payment.
  • We are unable to complete the project due to circumstances on our side.

3.2 When Refunds Are Not Provided

Refunds are typically not available when:

  • Work has started or significant time has already been invested.
  • You change direction or decide to cancel after receiving concepts or drafts.
  • Delays or issues occur due to missing feedback, missing content, or unresponsiveness.
  • You are dissatisfied based on personal preference when work aligns with the approved scope and reasonable revision process.

4. Cancellations by the Client

If you choose to cancel a project:

  • You are responsible for payment for all work completed up to the cancellation date.
  • No license or ownership is granted for unpaid work, drafts, or concepts.
  • At our discretion, we may offer a partial refund for uncompleted work, based on the proportion of the project already completed.

5. Cancellations by Elevated AI Works

We may cancel a project if, for example:

  • We do not receive necessary information or feedback within a reasonable time.
  • Communication becomes excessively delayed or unresponsive.
  • We encounter conflicts of interest, ethical issues, or other concerns.

In such cases, we may provide a partial refund for any work not yet performed, at our reasonable discretion.

6. Revisions & Scope Changes

Revision rounds are outlined in your proposal. Major changes in direction or scope (for example, a new brand concept after approval) may require a new quote and additional fees. Scope changes do not qualify for refunds of work already completed.

7. Digital Deliverables

Because our work is digital, once final files or deliverables have been provided, they cannot be “returned.” As a result, refunds are generally not available after final deliverables have been delivered, except in rare circumstances determined by us.

8. How to Request a Cancellation or Refund Review

To request a cancellation or refund review, please contact us in writing with your name, project details, and a brief description of your request. We’ll review the situation and respond within a reasonable time.
